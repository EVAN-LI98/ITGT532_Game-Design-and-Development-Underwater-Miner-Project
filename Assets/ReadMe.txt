----ENG

Operation guide

When the character is still, three animations will be played, which are idle--Stare blankly1--Stare blankly2.
WASD control character movement
Shift+WASD running
Left click, left sword attack
Right click, right sword attack
Space ---- jump
O ---- death
P ---- reborn
Q  ---- dodge left (only in movement\walk state)
E  ---- dodge right (only in movement\walk state)
X  ---- back dodge (only in movement\walk state)
F\R linkage skills (some specific skills used)
2-Skill 2 (can also be used in Switch postures state(Tab))
3-Skill 3
4-Skill 4 (Use 4 again during the skill period to link 4-2 skills or press F to link 8 skills)
5-Skill 5 (press F during skill 5 to link 6 skills)
6-Skill 6 (can also be used in Switch postures state(Tab) and can be triggered by skill 5)
7-Skill 7 (can also be used in Switch postures state(Tab) Press and hold R during Skill 7 to link Skill 7-2 for quick transfer)
Can only be used in Switch postures(Tab key) with skill 8 by pressing 8 in the Tab state or pressing F during skill 4.
Hold C key ---- crawl. Release C back to idle
Hold down the Z key ----- sit down. Release Z back to idle.
Press and hold the V key ----- dance. Release V back to idle.
Press and hold 1 ----- Defense Block. Release 1 back to idle.
Press and hold TAB ---- To switch postures. Release Tab back to idle.
